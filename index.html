Fosse Fibre

Who we are

Fosse Fibre is a group of spinners, knitters, weavers, felters, dyers and associated crafters based in Leicestershire. We meet monthly at the Attenborough Arts Centre to demonstrate techniques, work on projects together or just have a chat. New members welcome!
